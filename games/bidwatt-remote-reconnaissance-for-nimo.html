🏗️ What BidWatt Is

BidWatt is your full-stack bid management web app built for Irex Argus, a mechanical insulation contractor operating across 5 branches (Pasco WA, Seattle WA, Portland OR, Phoenix AZ, Salt Lake City UT). It tracks bids through a Kanban pipeline, a spreadsheet-style Bid Board, a calendar view, and a role-based reporting layer for Estimators, Branch Managers, and Admins. The stack is Next.js 16 App Router + Supabase (PostgreSQL + RLS), styled with shadcn/ui and Tailwind v4. It was developed on Windows at C:\Dev\cwatt-bidboard and has never been deployed to Vercel — it runs locally only so far.
